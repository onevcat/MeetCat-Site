How It Works

Three steps, quiet and reliable.

01

Detect Meetings

All you need to do is open the Meet homepage (in a browser with the extension installed or in the desktop app) and make sure the next-meeting overlay appears.

02

Set Trigger Time

Choose when to open the meeting page based on your settings. Confirm mic and camera on the pre-join page. Once you enter it, the countdown starts.

03

Auto-Join

When the countdown ends, it auto-joins the meeting based on your settings. Say goodbye to late joins from forgotten meetings—start on time, every time.
